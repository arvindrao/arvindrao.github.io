TRADING HYP
by Arvind Rao

You trade the stock of Hypothetical Systems Corp (HYP) in this day trading simulation game.
Follow the price action, moving in and out of the market as it swings wildly!

Rules and tips

  1. You have infinite money.
  2. News will move the market. Keep a close eye on the news ticker.
  3. Watch the chat screen for helpful tips.
  4. Your orders are market orders.
  5. Short selling is permitted.
  6. Place orders in multiples of 100. The maximum order size is 4,000 shares.
  7. You may hold a maximum of 20,000 shares in a long or short position.
  8. The game never ends! Close out your positions for your final profit or loss score.
